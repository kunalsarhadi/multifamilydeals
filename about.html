The Asset Class

Why We Focus on 6–10 Plexes

Not condos. Not single family. Not 100-unit towers. The 6–10 plex is the most efficient vehicle for the MLI Select program — and the fastest path to 50 doors.

01
MLI Select Sweet Spot

CMHC's MLI Select program requires a minimum of 5+ units. We recommend the 6–10 plex as the optimal entry point — this is where 5% down and 50-year amortization delivers the strongest investor returns. Larger towers require institutional capital.

02
Maximum Leverage Per Dollar

A 6-plex at 5% down requires approximately ~$80,000–$90,000 as a deposit. A 10-plex requires approximately ~$140,000–$155,000. The same capital deployed in Ontario buys you one condo with 20% down. In Edmonton, it buys you 6–10 doors with projected positive cash flow from the start.

03
Fastest Path to 50 Doors

Each acquisition adds 6–10 doors at a time. With 5–6 acquisitions over 4 years, investors reach the 50-door threshold — the level at which property income becomes fully self-sustaining and refinancing unlocks further capital. Subject to CMHC approval, available financing, and market conditions at each stage.

04
Passive by Design

Unlike single-family rentals that demand hands-on management, 6–10 plex assets are professionally managed as a single entity. One management agreement. One monthly statement. Zero landlord calls.

Turn-Key Operations

How We Manage
From Any Province

Our investors live across Alberta and Ontario. Distance is not a barrier. Our turn-key structure was built specifically for out-of-province owners — and we have team members on the ground in Edmonton available for site tours and video walkthroughs on request.

01
Acquisition & Setup

We handle the full acquisition process — financing, legal, inspections, and title transfer. You sign documents remotely. Your property is tenanted and operational before your first mortgage payment. See the step-by-step process →

02
Professional Property Management

Our licensed property management team handles tenant relations, lease agreements, maintenance requests, and unit turnovers. Day-to-day operations do not require your involvement — your role is to review your monthly statement.

03
Monthly Reporting & Deposits

Rent is collected by the property management company, reconciled, and net cash flow is transferred to your account each month — along with a detailed income statement, occupancy report, and maintenance log. Full transparency, zero administration.

04
Annual Portfolio Review

Each year we review your portfolio performance, refinancing opportunities, and the next acquisition target on your 50-door roadmap. Your portfolio grows on a schedule — not by accident.
